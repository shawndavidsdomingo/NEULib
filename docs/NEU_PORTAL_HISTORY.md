# NEU Library Portal: Institutional Development History

This document chronicles the evolution of the NEU Library Portal from its inception to its current institutional deployment.

---

## 🏛 Project Vision
The NEU Library Portal is a sophisticated visitor management system designed for high-end academic oversight. It bridges the gap between physical identity simulation and digital attendance tracking with a focus on "Smart UI" aesthetics and scholarly integrity.

---

## 📅 Phase 1: Foundations & Terminal (Days 1-2)
**Goal**: Establish the core visitation logic.
- **Kiosk Implementation**: Created a dedicated `TerminalView` supporting RFID simulation and Google Institutional Login (@neu.edu.ph).
- **Identity Simulation**: Developed logic to automatically toggle between Check-In and Check-Out based on existing active logs.
- **Registry CRUD**: Built the initial Student Registry and Staff Access systems with role-based permissions.

## 📅 Phase 2: Analytics & Scholarly Pivot (Days 3-4)
**Goal**: provide institutional insights and student self-service.
- **Student Portal**: Added a dedicated dashboard for students to view their historical presence and monthly commitment.
- **Recharts Integration**: Implemented weekly and departmental analytics for administrative oversight.
- **Visual Refresh**: Pivoted from generic "green vibes" to professional **Institutional Blue** and introduced the stationary **neulibrary.jpg** background with frosted-glass surfaces.

## 📅 Phase 3: The "Midnight Cut-off" & "No Tap" Marking (Current)
**Goal**: ensure data integrity and automated reporting.
- **Midnight Logic**: Implemented a strict 12:00 AM cut-off. Any session not terminated by midnight is automatically identified as **"No Tap"** or **"Missed Out"**.
- **Security Update**: Restricted Administrative manual terminations; visitation logs must be strictly generated by physical or simulated student interactions at the Kiosk.
- **SWR Implementation**: Upgraded the Registry and Reporting layers with **Stale-While-Revalidate (SWR)** caching for maximum performance.
